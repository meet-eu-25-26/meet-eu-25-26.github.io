Course Description

Meet-EU is an international team-based course organised by six 4EU+ member universities, namely Copenhagen, Heidelberg, Milan, Paris, Prague, Warsaw, as part of the 4EU+ joint educational offer during the academic year 2025-2026.

Meet-EU has been designed to promote interdisciplinary training through research and welcomes students from computer science, physics, mathematics, chemistry, biology and biotechnologies. Students address an ambitious and open research problem in group of 4-5. The course ends with an inverse symposium where students present their work in front of a jury of internationally recognised researchers and the researchers present in turn recent works from their labs.

Coopetition

Work in teams within and across universities
